I'm a Full Stack Developer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. I've spent the last 5+ years building and shipping production web applications for ecommerce, SaaS, education, and B2B clients. My core expertise is the Python/Django and ecosystems, paired with React/Next.js on the frontend and AWS/Docker for deployment.

I treat every codebase as a product — readable, tested, and shippable to a live environment without ceremony. I work closely with product and design, communicate proactively, and care about the metrics my code moves.

My Journey

I started writing PHP at university, fell in love with the elegance of Django, and have since built marketplaces, learning platforms, internal HR tools, and high-throughput APIs. Alongside client work, I mentor 4,000+ students learning full stack development in Bangla.
